CAN bit timing configurations

    Each bit on the CAN bus is divided in to 4 segments
        -Synchronization Segment
        -Propogation Segment
        -Phase Segment 1
        -Phase Segment 2

    The width of these segments is alterered to get a desired bit rate
    on the CAN bus. This is defined in quanta

    The (TIME QUANTA) is the smallest time unit for all configuration values.
